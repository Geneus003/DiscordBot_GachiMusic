!gc!help - To  show all commands(works independent from prefix)
!gc!play <p> - To play local music, with number p  (!gc!play song_number)
!gc!list - To show all local songs  (!gc!list list_number)
!gc!random - To play random local music
!gc!stop - To stop playing song
!gc!leave - The bot will leave channel
!gc!prefix - Set up the prefix  (!gc!prefix !!)
!gc!playing_games - To play gachi game theme song if you start playing in correct game
